Course Selection Explanation

This course selection system is for MSCS students in the Computer Professionals track. At some point during the MPP course students will use this system to enter their preferences for the rest of their on-campus courses.

Assignment to classes will be according to

  • student preference
  • registration number (low to high for first and fourth blocks and high to low for second and third blocks)

Students must do the following

  • get all waivers, transfers and prerequisites approved before registering preferences
  • enter preferences for different course offerings
